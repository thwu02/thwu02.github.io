The Fortress

A mass of bare concrete and narrow window slits  
Looming in the mist  
Two sentinels eye me from across the channel  
A thousand rustling dark green eyes  
Vigilant against shadows in the grey  

When the sky returns from slate to azure  
And the walls glow from golden rays  
The sentries still to grant me pass  
To rest before my journey through  
Into the endless blue
